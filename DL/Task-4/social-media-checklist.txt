📋 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (for college students)
Do’s
- Use respectful and professional language when posting or commenting.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Keep accounts secure with strong, unique passwords.
- Maintain a positive digital footprint by sharing constructive content.
- Credit original sources when reposting or sharing content.
Don’ts
- Share personal or sensitive information publicly.
- Post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
- Engage in online arguments aggressively.
- Ignore privacy settings on social platforms.
- Spread rumors or unverified news.
